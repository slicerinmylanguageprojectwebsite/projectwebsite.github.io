3D slicer in my language

3D Slicer is an open-source software that specializes in segmentation, visualization and treatment planning for medical imaging. It is used by healthcare professionals and researchers around the world for research, teaching and clinical care. The software is developed by an international community of developers, clinicians and end users who work together to constantly improve the software and make it a powerful and user-friendly tool for healthcare professionals.

Participate to Dakar workshop

The objective of the 3D Slicer workshop in Dakar is to present the latest version of 3D Slicer, Slicer 5.0, to the scientific and medical community in French-speaking Africa. The workshop is organized in a series of workshops allowing participants to become familiar with DICOM data loading in Slicer, image segmentation and 3D visualization. All workshop sessions will be taught in French.
